About Us Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter Puerto Princesa City, Palawan

Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray animals. Located in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan, we assist adopters in finding new families for stray cats and dogs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and rescued animals, ensuring their well-being through advocacy and partnerships focused on animal welfare.

Vision: To create a compassionate future for every rescued animal.

 About PawsConnect

PawsConnect is a mobile and web app that helps adopters find their best match among the animals at Nativity's Shelter.
