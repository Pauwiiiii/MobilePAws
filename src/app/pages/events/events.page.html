Frequently Asked Questions Here are the most frequently asked questions about the events of the shelter.

What events does the shelter host?

We host a variety of events, including adoption fairs, fundraising galas, volunteer appreciation days, pet wellness clinics, and community outreach activities. These events are designed to support our mission and engage the community.

How can I find upcoming events?

You can find information about our upcoming events on the "Events" tab of our website, or follow us on social media for the latest updates.

How can I participate in an event?

Participation details are provided for each event on our website. You may need to register in advance, buy tickets, or sign up as a volunteer depending on the event.
